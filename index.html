Tamara Gutierrez

Student of Computing Engineering and M.Sc. Student in Computer Science at Computer Science Department (DCC), University of Chile, Santiago.

My research interests include software engineering, quality assurance and testing of embedded systems for space applications.

Since 2020, I am a M.Sc. student at University of Chile working with Alexandre Bergel on the study of systematic applications of fuzz testing techniques in the SUCHAI flight software. Also, I continue working as a developer and researcher at SPEL in the current missions of the SUCHAI nanosatellite program: PlantSat, SUCHAI II and SUCHAI III.
